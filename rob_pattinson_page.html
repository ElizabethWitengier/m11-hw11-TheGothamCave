Robert Pattinson as The Batman

Robert Pattinson took on one of the most globally recognizable characters with The Batman. At this point, the character has shown up close to a dozen times in live-action alone, played by actors ranging from Michael Keaton to Ben Affleck. In this 2022 release, the former Twilight star give life to Bruce Wayne. He has been absolutely tremendous, vanishing completely into the role and giving a version of the character that is both familiar and unique. Pattinson said he’d been a fan of the character since his youth and wanted to do unique things with the character’s superhero and civilian identities that other films hadn’t touched on before.
